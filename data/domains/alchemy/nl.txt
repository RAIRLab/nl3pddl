The alchemy domain models mixing and combining available ingredients to create potions in a step by step process. 
The alchemist collects ingredients, adds them to a container, mixes them, and brews a potion.

The domain includes the following actions:
1. collect-ingredient: an alchemist collects available ingredients, removing them from the available ingredients list.
2. add-ingredient: an alchemist adds an ingredient to a container.
3. clean-container: an alchemist cleans a container by removing an ingredient from it.
4. mix-ingredients: an alchemist mixes three different ingredients in a container to complete the mixture.
5. brew-potion: an alchemist brews a potion from a complete mixture in a container.
